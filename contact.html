About Us

We are a team of researchers from ICT 12-A at Laguna University. Our project, LUcator, aims to enhance campus navigation for new students by developing an interactive map of the university. Our objective is to create a comprehensive 2D mapping system that accurately depicts campus buildings, pathways, and landmarks to improve user experiences.
