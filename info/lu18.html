Human Resource Management Office

LU 18

Location

Laguna University - Annex Building

Purpose / Function

To ensure continuous personal and professional growth of Laguna University teaching and non-teaching personnel through endeavors that would optimize their capabilities/potentials toward the promotion of total human development.

Services

  • Involvement in the strategic planning process
  • Forecasting the labor requirement
  • Recruitment, Selection and Placement
  • Induction
  • Learning and Development
  • Performance Management System
  • Rewards and Recognition
  • Attendance Monitoring
  • 201-Files Management
  • Complaints, Grievance and Discipline
  • Retirement
