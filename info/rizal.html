Jose Rizal Monument

Location

Laguna Sports Complex, Brgy. Bubukal Santa Cruz Laguna

History

Unveiled in 2014 to coincide with the Palarong Pambansa held in Laguna that year, the Jose Rizal Monument at the Laguna Sports Complex offers a unique tribute to the national hero. Standing tall at 7.9 meters, it's the Philippines' highest Rizal statue, but more importantly, it depicts him not as a scholar or writer, but as a fencer, highlighting his lesser-known athletic prowess and aiming to inspire a more well-rounded view of Dr. Jose Rizal.
