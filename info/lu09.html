Senior High School
Faculty Room

Location

Laguna University - Annex Building

Office Hours

Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Purpose / Function

Serves as a central hub for teachers to collaborate on lesson planning, access resources, and complete administrative tasks. It facilitates communication among staff members, providing a space for important announcements and professional development opportunities. Additionally it offers a place for teachers to take breaks, relax, and socialize, contributing to a supportive and cohesive school environment.
